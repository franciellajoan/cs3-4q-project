Welcome to the Hybridization Simulator

This website teaches you the basics of the hybridization of the Oxygen atom.

What is the Hybridization Simulator

The Hybridization Simulator helps you understand the hybridization of the Oxygen atom. It lets you know the different kinds of hybrid orbitals and helps you visualize the atomic model of the hybridized and hybridized state of the atom.
