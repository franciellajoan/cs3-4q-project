How to use the Hybridization Simulator?

Step 1
Place the number of electron domain you want to put in the simulator. Note that oxygen can only hybridize between 3 to 4 electron domain.
Step 2
When you are really sure of the number of electron domain you want, click the submit button. wait for the 3d model and the new electronic configuration of the hybridized orbital.
Step 3
Analyze the data/view the results. Note that in 3 electron domain the p orbital is colored yellow while the sp orbitals are colored green. In the 4 electron domain all sp orbitals are colored green.
